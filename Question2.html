Uffizi Galleries

Official Website of Uffizi Galleries

The Uffizi Gallery is a prominent art museum located adjacent to the Piazza della Signoria in the Historic Centre of Florence in the region of Tuscany, Italy.

Getting tickets in advance is strongly recommended!

Trattoria Zà Zà

Website and Menu for Trattoria Zà Zà

Tuscan restaurant with a bright dining room as well as brick-walled & frescoed interior rooms.

My personal recommendation is to get the gnocci, a favorite economical option in an fantastic restaurant, but everything is good!

Boboli Gargens

Website for Boboli Gardens

Boboli Gardens is a historical park of the city of Florence that was opened to the public in 1766. Originally designed for the Medici, it represents one of the first and most important examples of the Italian garden, which later served as inspiration for many European courts. Statues of various styles and periods, ancient and Renaissance, dot the garden. It also has large fountains and artificial caves, notably a grotto built by the artist, architect, and sculptor Bernardo Buontalenti between 1536 and 1608.

Visit after lunch at Trattoria Zà Zà and nap in the sculpture garden on the terraced fields by the fountains!

Piazzale Michelangelo

A Guide to Piazzale Michelangelo

While it might not feature the original David, the view of Florence from Piazzale Michelangelo is worth the hike up the hill to this free park featuring cafes, vendors and restrooms.

Located not too far from Boboli Gardens, take a hike up the florentine hill to Piazzale Michelangelo and then enjoy one of the best views of Florence.
